About Us

We design intelligent spaces that balance innovation, functionality, and a visionary approach for tomorrow's living.

Hungchi International Ltd. specializes in the R&D, production and export of high-end integrated pods and modular houses — delivering one-stop smart space solutions for global clients across Europe, Asia-Pacific, Africa and the Americas.

What We Do
We design and manufacture modular structures, developing intelligent layouts, premium materials, and spatial solutions that support daily use and long-term comfort.
Our Approach
Our process is rooted in precision manufacturing, international certification, and collaborative service — from concept to global delivery.
