About Hungchi

Taiwan-Made.
World-Delivered.

Hungchi International was founded in Taiwan with one belief: every business deserves a space that works as hard as they do.

Since 2008, we have operated a 19,421 m² metal structure manufacturing facility, staffed by 210 skilled technicians and equipped with large laser cutting, bending, and plasma welding equipment.

Our products are exported to Europe, Asia-Pacific, Africa, and the Americas — and we are now actively expanding across Southeast Asia, with dedicated support for our Philippine partners.

With EU CE, UKCA and ISO certifications, a Grade 3A credit rating, and multiple product patents, we are the preferred long-term partner for overseas clients seeking quality and reliability.
